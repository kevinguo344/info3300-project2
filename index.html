Pitchfork vs. The People

By Boris Chan (bkc38), Kevin Guo (kg344), and Yue Shi (ys764)

Overview

Founded in 1999, Pitchfork is a music review website, scoring new music on a scale of 1 to 10. Pitchfork has become a tastemaker of the independent music scene, helping popularize artists such as Arcade Fire and Bon Iver. A glowing review could from the site could launch an unknown artist into stardom while a scathing review could destroy an acclaimed career in what is known as the Pitchfork Effect. This project is meant to explore not only Pitchfork's tastes over the years but also how aligned it is to the music tastes of the mainstream.
